## Types of Networks & Their Use Cases
### In today’s digital world, computer networks play a very important role in communication and data sharing. Different types of networks are used based on the size, coverage area, and purpose. Understanding these network types helps in designing efficient and secure communication systems.In this activity, I explored three major types of networks: LAN (Local Area Network), WAN (Wide Area Network), and PAN (Personal Area Network)
