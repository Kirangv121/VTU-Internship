## 🌐 Types of Networks

### 🏠 LAN (Local Area Network)
- Covers small areas (home, office)
- High speed and low latency

#### ✅ Use Cases:
- Office networks
- School labs
- Home Wi-Fi

---

### 🌍 WAN (Wide Area Network)
- Covers large geographical areas
- Connects multiple LANs

#### ✅ Use Cases:
- Internet
- Banking networks
- Enterprise branch connections

---

### 📱 PAN (Personal Area Network)
- Very short range network
- Connects personal devices

#### ✅ Use Cases:
- Bluetooth devices
- Smartwatches
- Wireless peripherals

---

## 📊 Comparison Table

| Feature        | LAN 🏠          | WAN 🌍           | PAN 📱          |
|----------------|----------------|------------------|----------------|
| Coverage       | Small          | Large            | Very Small     |
| Speed          | High           | Medium/Low       | Low            |
| Ownership      | Private        | Public/Private   | Personal       |
| Example        | Office Wi-Fi   | Internet         | Bluetooth      |
