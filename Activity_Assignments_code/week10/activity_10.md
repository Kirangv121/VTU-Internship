## 📡 Wireless Coverage Planning

### 🔹 Key Factors:
- Area size and layout
- Building structure (walls, floors)
- Number of users/devices
- Interference sources

---

## 📍 Access Point Placement Strategies

### 🔹 Best Practices:
- Place APs in central locations
- Avoid obstacles like walls and metal objects
- Mount APs on ceilings for better coverage
- Ensure overlapping coverage for seamless connectivity

---

### 🔹 Placement Types:
- **Grid Placement** – Even distribution
- **High-Density Placement** – For crowded areas
- **Edge Placement** – Covers boundary zones

---

## 📈 Scalability Considerations

### 🔹 Important Points:
- Easy addition of new APs
- Load balancing across devices
- Support for increasing users
- Use of cloud or centralized management
