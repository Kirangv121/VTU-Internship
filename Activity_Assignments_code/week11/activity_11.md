## 📧 Phishing Identification Checklist

### 🔹 Email Indicators:
- 🚫 Unknown or suspicious sender
- 🔗 Fake or mismatched URLs
- ⚠️ Urgent or threatening messages
- ❌ Poor grammar and spelling
- 📎 Unexpected attachments

---

## 🧠 Understanding Social Engineering

### 🔹 Common Techniques:
- 🎭 Impersonation (pretending to be trusted entities)
- 📞 Pretexting (creating fake scenarios)
- 🎁 Baiting (offering something attractive)
- 🕵️ Tailgating (unauthorized physical access)

---

## 📶 Recognizing Fake Wi-Fi Networks

### 🔹 Signs of Fake Networks:
- Open (no password) networks
- Similar names to trusted networks
- Unusual login pages
- Sudden disconnection/reconnection

---

## 🧩 Example Analysis

### 📌 Scenario: Phishing Email
- User receives email claiming to be from bank
- Contains urgent message and fake link

#### 🔍 Analysis:
- Sender email is not official
- Link redirects to fake website
- Requests sensitive information

---

### 📌 Scenario: Fake Wi-Fi
- User connects to "Free_Airport_WiFi"
- Attacker intercepts data

#### 🔍 Analysis:
- No encryption
- Network not verified
- Data exposed to attacker
