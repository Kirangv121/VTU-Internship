# 📅 April 13 – Cyber Security Learning Log

## ⚠️ Types of Network Attacks

### 🕵️ Industrial Espionage
**Industrial espionage** involves unauthorized access to confidential business information for competitive advantage.

#### 🔹 Examples:
- Stealing trade secrets
- Accessing confidential research data
- Corporate spying by competitors or insiders

#### 🔹 Impact:
- Loss of intellectual property
- Competitive disadvantage
- Financial losses

---

### 📂 Data Breaches
A **data breach** occurs when sensitive, confidential, or protected data is accessed or disclosed without authorization.

#### 🔹 Examples:
- Leakage of customer data (emails, passwords)
- Exposure of financial information
- Unauthorized database access

#### 🔹 Causes:
- Weak security controls
- Phishing attacks
- Insider threats

---

### 🚫 Denial-of-Service (DoS) Attacks
A **Denial-of-Service (DoS)** attack aims to make a network or service unavailable to users.

#### 🔹 How It Works:
- Overloads system with excessive traffic
- Exhausts server resources

#### 🔹 Types:
- DoS (single source)
- DDoS (Distributed from multiple sources)

#### 🔹 Impact:
- Website downtime
- Loss of revenue
- Poor user experience

---

## 📉 Impact of Cyberattacks on Organizations

### 🔹 Major Effects:
- 💸 **Financial Loss** – Recovery costs, legal penalties
- 📉 **Reputation Damage** – Loss of customer trust
- ⛔ **Operational Disruption** – Downtime and reduced productivity
- ⚖️ **Legal Consequences** – Non-compliance penalties
- 🔐 **Data Loss** – Exposure of sensitive information

---

## 🛡️ Network Resilience Strategies

Network resilience ensures that systems can withstand and recover from cyberattacks.

### 🔹 Key Strategies:
- 🔄 **Regular Backups** – Protect data from loss
- 🧱 **Firewalls & Security Systems** – Prevent unauthorized access
- 🔐 **Strong Encryption** – Secure data transmission
- 📊 **Monitoring & Incident Response** – Detect and respond quickly
- 🔁 **Redundancy** – Backup systems to ensure availability
- 🎓 **User Awareness Training** – Educate employees on security risks

---

## ✅ Summary
- Network attacks include industrial espionage, data breaches, and DoS attacks
- Cyberattacks can cause financial, operational, and reputational damage
- Resilience strategies help organizations prevent, detect, and recover from attacks
